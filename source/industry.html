Application of blockchain technologies in the dating industry

Dating apps are becoming an increasingly common way to meet new people. Some companies are trying to maintain interest in dating apps through technology, using blockchain and artificial intelligence for dating advice. Others may even accept cryptocurrencies as payment for using the platform.

Blockchain technology will make the online dating industry transparent. Already today, there are several platforms that, using this technology, are able to guarantee the integrity of online relationships:

  • Hicky

    It is one of the blockchain startups that is built to make this transformation happen. Hicky aims to bring credibility to the industry by leveraging the immutability of the blockchain. According to research, more than 50% of all people who have used online dating platforms have lied to the other person when communicating on the Internet. Lack of trust has made online dating not only frustrating but even dangerous. Hicky subjects new users to a rigorous vetting process to ensure that it fully matches its claimed identity. Identification will include voice checking and face scans. The decentralized offline dating (DAD) mechanism used will reward users with HKY tokens for good behavior and punish bad ones, which ultimately simplifies the use of the platform and makes it favorable for all participants.

  • Luna

    Luna is another blockchain-based solution designed to tackle the challenges of the online dating industry. Luna uses a constantly improving matching algorithm. The user is notified of the match and if he / she agrees to a meeting, both participants receive tokens as a reward. If the provided information about the identity turns out to be false, all received tokens are taken back. Luna's own cryptocurrency is also awarded to users who read and respond to messages in a timely manner. This makes the platform very interactive as it not only helps to find love, but also allows you to earn tokens that can be exchanged on cryptocurrency exchanges.
